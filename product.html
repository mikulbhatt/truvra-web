The platform

One neutral control plane for autonomous media buying.

Truvra sits between every agent in the ecosystem — buyer, seller, and the data, identity, measurement, and creative providers in between — independent of any single platform, making every action verifiable, authorized, governed, and provable. Identity vendors don't understand adtech. Verification vendors don't check the agent. Platforms build walls. Truvra closes the gap.

Modules

Six capabilities, grouped into three planes.

Control plane · define & verify

VerifyTruvra Identity

Every agent in a transaction gets a cryptographic, attestable identity bound to the party it acts for. Counterparties can confirm — in milliseconds — that an agent is genuine and authorized to be at the table, closing the "who is this agent?" gap that generic IAM can't answer for adtech.

AuthorizeTruvra Policy

An ontology-driven rule engine that encodes what agents are and what they may do: permitted actions, deal terms, budget ceilings, and brand-safety standards — expressed once and enforced everywhere, instead of re-implemented per platform.

Runtime · decide & gate

AuthorizeTruvra Signal

Authority isn't static. Signal evaluates the live context of each request — advertiser, inventory, audience, market conditions — and grants the right authority at the right moment, so governance adapts instead of blocking.

GovernTruvra Runtime

The enforcement gate. Agent actions pass through Runtime, which lets them proceed only within authorized pacing and budget bounds. Anything outside scope is held for review — not silently bought.

Assurance · record & show

ProveTruvra Ledger

A tamper-evident, queryable record of every authorized action — agent, authority, context, and outcome. The system of record that makes autonomous spend defensible to brands, finance, risk, and regulators.

ProveTruvra Lens

Real-time observability across agents, policies, and outcomes. See what every agent did, why it was allowed, and under whose authority — in dashboards built for both operators and auditors.

Neutral by design

Protocol-native. Platform-independent.

Truvra is built to extend open agent standards like the Ad Context Protocol, and to work across DSPs, SSPs, and retail-media platforms — not to lock you into one stack. The trust, authority, and audit trail travel with the agent, wherever it transacts.

  • Verifies any agent role — buy side, sell side, or intermediary
  • Extends open protocols rather than replacing them
  • One identity & audit trail across every counterparty
  • Model- and platform-agnostic by default
  • Bring your own policies, deals, and brand standards

The reasoning & context layer

An ontology- and graph-driven core.

Beneath the modules, Truvra runs on a knowledge graph and ontology that make the meaning of every agent, audience, deal, and signal machine-readable. As real-time bidding gives way to real-time reasoning, that's what lets governance track semantic alignment between agents — then certify it independently, and, as a value-add, power a closed learning loop.
