Company

We're building the trust layer for the agentic economy — starting with media.

Truvra is a Ritava company. We believe autonomous agents will transact at the heart of the economy within this decade — and that they can only do so if every action is verifiable, authorized, and accountable. We're starting where the volume, the money, and the urgency are highest: media buying.

"Every wave of automation eventually meets the same question: can you trust what the machine just did? Agentic media buying is hitting that wall now. Truvra exists to answer it — to make autonomy something brands, publishers, and regulators can verify, not just hope for."

— The Truvra founding team

What we believe

Principles we're building on.

Neutral beats walled

Trust can't come from one platform grading its own homework. The trust layer has to be independent — Switzerland for agent transactions.

Verify the actor

A decade of adtech verified the impression. Agentic buying introduces a new unverified actor: the agent itself. That's the gap we close.

Governance enables

Done right, governance isn't a brake — it's what lets autonomous spend move from pilot to production with confidence.

The company

Truvra, a Ritava company.

Truvra is the first product of Ritava, Inc. — a company building trust and governance infrastructure for autonomous systems. Media buying is where we start; the control plane is built to extend wherever agents transact next.

The name. Ritava draws on Ṛta (ऋत) — the ancient idea of cosmic order and truth, the unseen structure by which things hold together, and by which the sun rises on time, every day. Truvra carries that idea into a new domain: verifiable truth and order for autonomous agents. Order before action.
